Participate / Submit

Submit a run

Official submissions open with the first benchmark version. Today you can submit results on the synthetic pilot harness, or request a private evaluation of your system. Submissions are reviewed before appearing on the leaderboard.

How submission works

  1. Run your system under walk-forward sealing on the chosen track/split.
  2. Produce a sealed results file (timestamped + hashed) and a metadata record.
  3. Submit the form below. Your entry lands as pending review.
  4. The organizer triages it to public, verified, or private-eval (or requests changes).

Submission tiers

  • Public (unofficial) — self-reported on the open/synthetic split. Clearly labeled.
  • Verified — the organizer reproduces your result from submitted code.
  • Private eval — the organizer runs your system on a frozen hidden split end to end.
⛔

No future leakage. Only evidence timestamped at or before the forecast time t may inform a forecast. Random cross-validation, peeking at outcomes, or training on the resolution window invalidates a submission.

Required files & metadata

  • Results file (sealed forecasts), or a runnable system for private eval.
  • Model/system name, output adapter, training cutoff.
  • Track, split, and benchmark version.
  • Both attestations below.
